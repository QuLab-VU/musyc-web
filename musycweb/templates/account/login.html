MuSyC

Multidimensional Synergy of Combinations


MuSyC is an algorithm for quantifying drug effect synergy. It distinguishes synergy of efficacy from synergy of potency.

This service allows you upload your own data to produce fit parameters and examine dose-response surfaces. It is free for non-commercial use, subject to terms and conditions.
